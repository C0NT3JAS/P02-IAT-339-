Branding Ideas

The company offers public spaces themed after winter/cool colors for leisure, work, or studying, while selling hot or cold drinks with sides to enjoy while doing so.

To potential customers, the diner is a relaxation spot and eatery with branding and themes that evoke the same cozyness that comes with the winter season.

One major reason a customer might feel enticed to purchase products from the company would be the option of enjoying them in the diner itself, or ordering certain ingredients for making them at home

Branding Components

Potential fonts used for the site would be softer/minimalistic to fit with the cafe's relaxation based atmosphere

Colour Palettes and art direction for the website would aim for cooler colors, with combinations of blue and white to represent the winter theming
